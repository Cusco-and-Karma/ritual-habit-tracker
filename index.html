How to connect (native app)
1
Download the Garmin Connect app on your phone and sign in to your Garmin account.
2
Make sure your Garmin device is synced to Garmin Connect at least once a day.
3
When the Ritual native app launches, tap Connect → Garmin to authorize access via Garmin Health API.
4
Your health data will automatically appear in this Health tab — no manual entry needed.
Note: Garmin's Health API requires OAuth and a server-side backend, which means the connection must go through the Ritual native app (coming soon). The web version will display your synced data once connected.
